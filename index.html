Research process

Systematic coverage, manual underwriting.

The edge is not a single model. It is the combination of broad systematic coverage, primary local-language sources, and disciplined hand underwriting of securities most desks ignore.

1

Screen broadly

A proprietary pipeline ranks approximately 69,000 global small and micro caps across roughly 156 countries into a ranked top-100 watchlist. View the live watchlist →

2

Source locally

Survivors are read against primary local-language filings and disclosures, not just English summaries.

3

Underwrite by hand

NAV / SOTP, forward order-book reconstruction, comparable valuation, catalysts, and bear/base/bull with downside.

4

Publish on the record

Theses are timestamped publicly, so the work can be judged on dated evidence rather than assertion.
